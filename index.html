My Process

I commit to delivering completed projects that not only meet, but exceed, my clients' initial vision. It is critical to understand what to expect when we collaborate; here is how I deliver success:

1. Consultation - It all starts with a conversation in which we will define the scope of this project.
2. Proposal - A detailed project proposal will be presented, along with a visual preview, and a plan of action will be decided.
3. Creation - Clients are kept up to date on the status of their projects as I create and test them.
4. Launch & Support - The final project includes peace of mind in the form of available ongoing support.
